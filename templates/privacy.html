Privacy Policy

Acquacotta ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Pomodoro time tracking application.

Privacy by Design: Acquacotta uses a stateless server architecture. This means our server never stores your pomodoro data, settings, or Google credentials. Your data lives only in your browser and optionally in your own Google Sheets.

Information We Collect

When you use Acquacotta, we may collect:

  • Account Information: If you sign in with Google, we receive your email address, name, and profile picture from Google. This information is stored only in your browser, not on our server.
  • Pomodoro Data: Time tracking entries you create, including task names, types, durations, timestamps, and notes.
  • Settings: Your application preferences and configurations.

Where Your Data Is Stored

Data Type Demo Mode (Not Logged In) Logged In Mode
Pomodoro entries Your browser only (IndexedDB) Your browser + Your Google Sheet
Settings Your browser only (IndexedDB) Your browser + Your Google Sheet
Google credentials N/A Your browser only (NOT on our server)

Stateless Server Architecture

Our server is designed to be completely stateless:

  • No database: We do not operate any database that stores your information.
  • No user data storage: Pomodoro entries, settings, and preferences are never stored on our server.
  • No credential storage: Your Google OAuth tokens are stored in your browser's IndexedDB, not on our server. They are sent with each request and used only to communicate with Google's APIs on your behalf.
  • Proxy only: Our server acts as a proxy between your browser and Google's APIs, never retaining any data.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the Acquacotta service
  • Authenticate you with Google (via OAuth)
  • Proxy requests to Google Sheets API on your behalf
  • Display your profile information within the app

Google Sheets Integration

When you log in with Google and connect to Google Sheets:

  • A spreadsheet titled "Acquacotta Pomodoro Tracker" is created in your Google Drive
  • This spreadsheet is owned by you and stored in your Google account
  • We request only the minimum permissions needed: access to files we create and your basic profile information
  • You can view, edit, or delete this spreadsheet at any time in Google Drive
  • Data syncs bidirectionally between your browser and your spreadsheet

Third-Party Services

Acquacotta uses the following third-party services:

  • Google OAuth: For authentication
  • Google Sheets API: For cloud data synchronization
  • Google Drive API: To create and locate your Acquacotta spreadsheet

These services are governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

What We Do NOT Collect

  • No analytics: We do not track how you use the app
  • No telemetry: We do not collect usage statistics
  • No cookies for tracking: We only use a session cookie for OAuth flow security (CSRF protection)
  • No third-party trackers: No advertising or analytics scripts

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties. Your pomodoro data remains private to you. Since we don't store your data, we couldn't share it even if we wanted to.

Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your information:

  • All communications use HTTPS encryption
  • OAuth tokens are stored only in your browser's IndexedDB
  • The server processes requests statelessly without retaining data
  • Google OAuth tokens are refreshed securely when needed

Offline Functionality

Acquacotta works offline:

  • All data is stored locally in your browser's IndexedDB
  • Changes made offline are queued and synced when you're back online
  • You can use the app without any internet connection in demo mode

CSV Import & Export

Acquacotta supports importing and exporting your data as CSV files:

CSV Export (Download)

  • You can download all your pomodoro data as a CSV file at any time
  • The export is generated entirely in your browser from your local IndexedDB
  • No data is sent to any server during export
  • The CSV file is saved directly to your device
  • CSV format includes: id, name, type, start_time, end_time, duration_minutes, notes

CSV Import (Upload)

  • You can import pomodoro data from a CSV file
  • The CSV file is processed entirely in your browser - it is never uploaded to our server
  • Imported entries are saved to your local IndexedDB
  • If logged in, imported entries sync to your Google Sheet
  • Duplicate entries (matching IDs) are automatically skipped
  • Your original CSV file remains on your device and is not retained by the app

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data: View all your data in the app or in your Google Sheet
  • Export your data: Download as CSV at any time
  • Delete your data: Clear local data from Settings, or delete entries from your Google Sheet
  • Disconnect Google: Log out to stop syncing; your Google Sheet remains yours
  • Revoke access: Remove Acquacotta's access in your Google Account settings

Data Retention

  • Browser data: Persists until you clear your browser data or use the "Clear All Data" option
  • Google Sheets data: Persists in your Google Drive until you delete the spreadsheet
  • Server data: None retained - server is stateless
